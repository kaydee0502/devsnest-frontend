Rogue Ninja

大蛇丸

Orochimaru (大蛇丸, Orochimaru) is one of Konohagakure's legendary Sannin. With a life-ambition to learn all of the world's secrets, Orochimaru seeks immortality so that he might live all of the lives necessary to accomplish his task.

Background

Orochimaru was once an orphan who became a pupil of Hiruzen Sarutobi alongside Jiraiya and Tsunade. Compared to the more laid back Jiraiya, Orochimaru stood out as a genius — his talents, knowledge, and determination were considered by Hiruzen to be that of a prodigy seen only once in a generation

Kekkai genkai

Sakon and Ukon's Kekkei Genkai, Wood Release, Jūgo's Clan's Kekkei Genkai, Shikotsumyaku

Orochimaru

Sanin

Abilities

Orochimaru has large reserves of strong chakra, powerful enough to stir up winds and crack stone. Likewise, the intensity of his killing intent can reduce most foes petrified in fear. He can absorb chakra through physical contact, which also grants him their skills and knowledge.

Affiliations

Akatsuki, Hidden Leaf Village, Otogakure, Root, Ryūchi Cave

Summoning

Orochimaru has an affinity for snakes, using the unique arm tattoo to summon them for assistance in battle, his largest and strongest being Manda.
